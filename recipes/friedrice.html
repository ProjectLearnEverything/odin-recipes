Fried Rice Recipe

Description

I love fried rice, who doesn't? In this recipe I will show you how anyone can whip up a fried rice which will surely impress the crowd and be loved by anyone!

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Egg
  • Garlic
  • Onion
  • Soy Sauce
  • Spam

Steps

  1. chop onion and garlic finely
  2. cook the rice in the mean time
  3. fry the onion first until translucent then garlic
  4. add the rest of the ingredients with the soy sauce
